Who Are We?

UBC AeroDesign is a student led engineering team at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada. The two primary purposes our team are:

  • To provide a learning environment for students to experiment and experience what it is like to work on a world class engineering design challenge in the field of aerospace.
  • To produce the most competitive and innovative designs for international university competition.
Our team is entirely student led, members are free to explore their interests and are encouraged to share skills and knowledge with each other. Junior members have the oppurnity to explore topics including aerodynamics, structures, manufacturing, electrical design, and software design (Solidworks and XFLR-5). In turn, senior members gain valuable leadership, presentation, and project management skills.
